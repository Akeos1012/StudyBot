**Data Replication** – Data replication is the **process of copying and maintaining the same data across multiple databases, servers, or locations to ensure consistency, availability, and reliability**.

It is commonly used in large-scale systems to prevent data loss and improve performance.

- Copies [[Data]] from one system to another
- Keeps multiple [[Database]] systems synchronized
- Improves [[Fault Tolerance]] and system reliability
- Enhances availability in [[Distributed Systems]]
- Reduces latency by placing data closer to users
- Used in [[Cloud Computing]] and backup systems

Data replication is the process of duplicating data across multiple systems to ensure consistency, availability, and durability.
