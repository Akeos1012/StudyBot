**Bluetooth** - A **short-range wireless communication technology standard** that allows devices to exchange data over radio waves without using cables.

It is designed for low power consumption and is commonly used to connect nearby devices for data transfer and communication. Bluetooth is a wireless system that lets devices connect and share data over short distances.

- Uses short-range radio frequency communication
- Connects devices without wires
- Automatically pairs devices within range
- Maintains low power usage
- Supports secure data transmission
#### How it Works?

Device A → Bluetooth Signal → Device B → Data Exchange → Connection Established
