**Digital Circuit** – An **electronic circuit that processes digital signals (0s and 1s) to perform logical operations and compute data in computers and electronic devices**.

- Processes [[Binary]] data (0 and 1)
- Performs logical operations using [[Logic Gates]]
- Forms the foundation of [[CPU]] and [[GPU]]
- Enables computation inside all modern computers
- Works using electrical signals (high = 1, low = 0)

#### How it works?

Digital circuits use voltage levels:

```
High voltage → 1Low voltage  → 0
```

These signals pass through components like:

- [[Transistor]] (basic switch)
- [[Logic Gate]] (AND, OR, NOT)
- [[Flip-Flop]] (memory element)

In short, a Digital Circuit is a hardware system that processes binary signals using logic gates and electronic components to perform computations in computers and digital devices.
