**Control Systems** – A control system is a **mechanism or set of components that manages, regulates, and directs the behavior of another system or device to achieve a desired output automatically or semi-automatically**.

- Controls the behavior of System outputs based on input conditions
- Uses feedback from sensors to adjust performance
- Common in automation and electronic devices
- Works closely with [[Embedded Systems]] and hardware controllers
- Can operate in real-time environments
- Often uses feedback loops to maintain stability

In short, A control system is a mechanism that regulates and manages the behavior of a system automatically or semi-automatically to achieve a desired output using feedback and control logic.
