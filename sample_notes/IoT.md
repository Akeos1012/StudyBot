**IoT** – (Internet of Things) is a computing concept where **physical devices are connected through networks to collect, exchange, process, and sometimes act on data automatically with minimal human intervention**. IoT combines sensors, connectivity, software, and computing systems to connect the physical world with digital systems.

Unlike traditional computing where humans directly operate devices, IoT enables devices to **communicate with each other, monitor environments, and trigger actions automatically based on incoming data**.

IoT is a network of connected physical devices that collect and exchange data to automate monitoring and decision-making.


#### How IoT works?

Physical Environment  
↓  
Sensors collect data  
↓  
Network Communication  
↓  
Data Processing  
↓  
Decision / Action  
↓  
Device Response

IoT devices usually collect information through sensors, transmit it through networks, process it locally or in cloud systems, then respond automatically or provide information to users.
