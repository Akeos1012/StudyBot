**Business Intelligence (BI)** – Business Intelligence refers to the **process, technologies, and systems used to collect, analyze, and transform raw data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making**. It focuses on turning large volumes of structured and unstructured data into reports, dashboards, and analytics that help organizations understand performance, trends, and opportunities.

Business Intelligence is built on top of data systems and analytical processes that convert [[Data]] into actionable knowledge for strategic and operational decision-making.

#### How System Works?

[[Data]] → Collection Systems → Data Storage ([[Database]] / Data Warehouse) → Processing ([[ETL Process]]) → Analysis ([[Statistics]] / [[Data Mining]]) → Visualization ([[Dashboard]]) → Decision Making


In short, Business Intelligence is the process of converting raw data into meaningful insights using analytics, systems, and visualization tools to support better business decision-making.
