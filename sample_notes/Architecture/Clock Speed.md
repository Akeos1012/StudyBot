**Clock Speed** – Clock speed refers to the **rate at which a computer’s CPU executes cycles or instructions per second**, determining how fast the processor can perform basic operations. It is typically measured in Hertz (Hz), with modern processors commonly operating in gigahertz (GHz), meaning billions of cycles per second.

Clock speed is a fundamental property of the processor’s timing system and is often used as a general indicator of CPU performance, although real-world speed also depends on architecture, cores, and efficiency.

#### How System Works?
