**Statistics** - A branch of mathematics that focuses on **collecting, organizing, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data** to extract meaningful information and support decision-making.

It provides the methods used to understand patterns, trends, uncertainty, and relationships within data. Statistics is the science of using data to describe, analyze, and make decisions about real-world situations.

#### Key areas of Statistics

#### Descriptive Statistics

- Summarizes and describes data
- Averages, charts, distributions
### Inferential Statistics

- Makes predictions based on sample data
- Generalizes results to larger populations

___
Statistics is a foundation of modern Information Technology because it allows systems to:

- analyze large-scale data in [[Big Data]] systems
- improve accuracy in [[Machine Learning]] models
- detect patterns in [[Data Mining]] processes
- support decision-making in AI systems
- measure performance and reliability of systems
- optimize algorithms and system efficiency
- power analytics in cloud-based platforms
