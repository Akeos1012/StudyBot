**Data preprocessing** - The **process of preparing raw data into a clean, structured, and usable format before it is used for analysis or machine learning models**.

It is one of the most important steps in Data Science and [[Machine Learning]] because raw data is often messy, incomplete, or inconsistent.

- Converts raw [[Data]] into a usable format
- Includes steps like [[Data Cleaning]], [[Data Integration]], and [[Data Transformation]]
- Handles missing values, duplicates, and incorrect entries
- Normalizes or standardizes data for consistency
- Improves performance of [[Machine Learning]] models
- Common step in [[Data Pipeline]] workflows

The preparation stage that ensures raw data is clean, consistent, and ready for analysis or modeling.
