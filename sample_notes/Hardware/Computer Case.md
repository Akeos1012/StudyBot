**Computer Case** – A computer case is the physical enclosure that **houses and protects all internal hardware components** of a computer system.

A computer case is the box that holds and protects all internal parts of the computer.

- Holds all components like **[[Motherboard]]**, **[[CPU]]**, **[[GPU]]**, and **[[Storage Devices]]**
- Protects hardware from dust, damage, and physical impact
- Supports airflow and cooling through fans and ventilation
- Organizes internal components for easier assembly and upgrades
#### Cooling and airflow

**The case is designed to:**
- Allow cool air in
- Push hot air out
- Support fans or liquid cooling systems

Good airflow = better performance and longer hardware life.

In short, a computer case is the protective enclosure that contains, organizes, and cools all internal computer hardware components.
