**Data Stream** – A Data Stream is a **continuous flow of data that is generated, transmitted, and processed in real time or near real time from one or more sources**.

- Represents continuously generated [[Data]]
- Processed in real time or near real time
- Comes from sources like sensors, apps, or online systems
- Used in [[Big Data]] and real-time analytics systems
- Requires fast [[Data Processing]] to handle incoming flow
- Often used in [[Edge Computing]] and [[Cloud Computing]] environments

**Example:**
- Live stock market prices
- Social media feeds (tweets, posts)
- Sensor data from IoT devices
- Live video or audio streams
- Network traffic logs

In short, a Data Stream is a continuous flow of data generated and processed in real time or near real time from one or more sources.
