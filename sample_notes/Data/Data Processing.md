**Data Processing** – Data processing is the **collection, transformation, and manipulation of raw data into meaningful and usable information through a series of steps performed by a computer system or software**.

- Converts raw [[Data]] into structured information
- Involves sorting, filtering, calculating, and organizing data
- Uses [[Algorithms]] to perform operations efficiently
- Happens in [[Database]] systems, applications, and operating systems
- Can be manual, automated, or real-time processing
- Essential for [[Data Analytics]] and decision-making systems
#### How System Works?

Raw Data → Input → Processing (Sorting, Calculating, Filtering) → Output → Information

In short, Data Processing is the process of collecting and transforming raw data into meaningful information through a series of computational steps performed by a system or software.
