**Deep Learning** – Deep learning is a **branch of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and a subset of Machine Learning that uses multi-layered artificial neural networks to learn patterns from large amounts of data and make predictions or decisions automatically**.

- Uses layered [[Neural Network]] architectures to learn complex patterns
- Learns automatically from large [[Dataset]] collections instead of manually coded rules
- Requires significant computational resources such as [[GPU]] and specialized hardware
- Supports automated feature extraction from raw [[Data]]
- Improves performance through repeated training and optimization
- Commonly used in image recognition, language processing, and prediction systems

Deep Learning → The system learns patterns mostly by itself from huge amounts of examples

#### How System Works?

In short, Deep Learning is an artificial intelligence approach that uses multi-layer neural networks to automatically learn patterns from large datasets and perform advanced prediction, recognition, and generation tasks.
