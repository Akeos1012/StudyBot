**[[AI]]** - (Artificial Intelligence) is a branch of computer science that focuses on building systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning from experience, recognizing patterns, understanding language, interpreting data, making decisions, and adapting to new inputs.

Unlike traditional systems that rely on fixed, rule-based instructions, AI systems operate by **learning from data**, identifying statistical patterns, and constructing internal representations (models) that allow them to generate predictions or decisions.

At its core, AI is not a single technology but a **multi-layered computational ecosystem**, where intelligence emerges from the interaction between data, learning algorithms, mathematical foundations, and system infrastructure.

The **AI system** is structured in interconnected layers, where each layer contributes to how intelligence is formed, processed, and deployed.
