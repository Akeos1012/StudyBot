**Intel** – A company that produces CPUs for computers, widely used in PCs and laptops.

**Intel CPUs often focus on:**
- High single-core responsiveness
- Hybrid architecture (**Performance Cores + Efficiency Cores**)
- Strong performance for mixed workloads
- Widely used in laptops, office PCs, and creator workloads

**Typical usage levels:**
- **Basic Use** → web browsing, documents, school
    - Intel Core 3 / Core 5
- **Mid-Level**
    - programming
    - video editing
    - multitasking
    - content creation
    - Intel Core 7
- **High-End**
    - rendering
    - simulation
    - heavy production workloads
    - Intel Core 9 / Xeon
