**Chip Fabrication** – Chip fabrication refers to the **manufacturing process of creating integrated circuits (microchips) from raw semiconductor materials, usually silicon**, through highly precise and controlled industrial processes.

Chip fabrication is the foundation of all modern computing hardware, since it produces the actual processors and memory chips used in devices like [[CPU]], [[GPU]], and [[RAM]].
