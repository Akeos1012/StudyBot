**Dataset** – A dataset is a **structured collection of data used to train, test, and evaluate systems such as [[Machine Learning]] models and other data-driven applications**.

It represents the raw material that AI and computing systems learn from. It is organized collection of data used for analysis, learning, and decision-making in computing systems.

**It contains:** 
- Raw or processed data
- Examples, records, or observations
- Structured or unstructured information
- Labels (in supervised learning cases)

### Types of dataset structure

**Structured Data**
- Organized in [[Table]] format
- Rows and columns
 
 **Unstructured Data**
- Images, audio, text, video
- No fixed format

**Semi-Structured Data**
- Mixed format 

#### Where datasets are used?

- [[Machine Learning]]
- [[Deep Learning]]
- [[Data Mining]]
- [[Big Data]]
- [[AI]]

#### Why datasets are important?

- Foundation of all AI training
- Determines model accuracy and performance
- Used for testing and validation
- Helps systems learn patterns from real-world information

In short, a dataset is a structured collection of data used to train, test, and evaluate machine learning and AI systems so they can learn patterns and make predictions.
