**Enterprise Computing** – Enterprise computing is the **use of large-scale computing systems, software, and infrastructure to manage, process, and store data for organizations such as businesses, governments, and institutions**.

- Supports large-scale [[Data]] processing and storage
- Uses centralized and distributed Systems
- Runs critical business applications like finance, HR, and logistics
- Depends on [[Cloud Computing]] and on-premise servers
- Focuses on security, reliability, and scalability
- Integrates multiple software and hardware systems together

In short, Enterprise Computing is the use of large-scale computing systems and infrastructure to support and manage business operations, data, and services for organizations efficiently and securely.
