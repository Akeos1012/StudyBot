**Data Analytics** – Data analytics is the **process of examining, cleaning, transforming, and interpreting data to discover useful patterns, insights, and support decision-making**.

- Involves collecting and processing [[Data]] from different sources
- Finds patterns, trends, and relationships in data
- Uses statistical methods and computational tools
- Supports decision-making in businesses, systems, and applications
- Works closely with Data Science and [[Algorithms]]
- Often uses tools like dashboards, reports, and visualization systems

Data analytics is the practice of analyzing raw data to extract valuable insights for decision-making and problem-solving.

In short, Data Analytics is the process of examining and interpreting data to find patterns, insights, and useful information that supports decision-making.
