**Actuator** – An actuator is a **hardware component in systems like [[IoT]] and robotics that converts electrical signals into physical movement or action**.

It is the part of a system that _does something in the real world_ based on instructions from a controller.
An actuator is a device that turns digital commands into physical action.

- Moves mechanical parts
- Opens or closes systems
- Controls physical motion or force
- Executes actions based on sensor or program input

#### Common types of actuators

- [[Electric Motor]]
- [[Servo Motor]]
- [[Stepper Motor]]
- [[Hydraulic System]]
- [[Pneumatic System]]
#### Where actuators are used

- Robotics
- [[IoT]] devices
- Smart Home systems
- Industrial Automation
- Automotive Systems
