**Cybersecurity** – A **practice of protecting computers, networks, systems, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, damage, or theft**.

#### What cybersecurity protects

- [[Computer]] systems
- [[Network]] infrastructure
- [[Data]] and sensitive information
- [[Software]] applications
- [[Server]] and [[Cloud Computing]] environments

#### Main goals (CIA Triad)

- **Confidentiality** → only authorized users can access data
- **Integrity** → data is not altered or corrupted
- **Availability** → systems stay accessible when needed

# ⚔️ Common threats

- [[Malware]] (viruses, worms, trojans)
- [[Phishing]] attacks
- [[Ransomware]]
- [[Denial of Service Attack]] (DoS / DDoS)
- [[Buffer Overflow]] exploits
- [[Man-in-the-Middle Attack]]

---

# 🛡️ Protection methods

- [[Encryption]]
- [[Firewall]]
- [[Authentication]]
- [[Access Control]]
- [[Antivirus Software]]
- [[Intrusion Detection System]]

In short, cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage.
