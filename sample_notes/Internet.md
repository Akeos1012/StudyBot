**Internet** – The Internet is a **global system of interconnected computer networks** that allows devices to communicate and exchange data using standardized communication protocols.

It connects billions of devices worldwide, enabling access to information, communication services, and online systems. The Internet is a worldwide network of computers that communicate and share data.

- Connects devices across the world
- Transfers data between systems
- Supports communication and information sharing
- Enables online services and applications
- Allows access to distributed systems

Provides the computing environment and distributed resources that support internet services at scale.

- [[Server]]
- [[Data Center]]
- [[Cloud Computing]]

In short, the Internet is a global communication network that connects devices and systems, enabling data exchange, online services, and access to digital resources worldwide.
