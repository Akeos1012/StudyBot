**Audio Processing** – The **manipulation, analysis, and transformation of sound signals (audio data) using software or hardware systems**.

- Converts sound waves into Digital Signal data
- Enhances or modifies audio quality
- Removes noise and distortion
- Mixes multiple audio sources
- Applies effects (echo, reverb, filters)
- Outputs sound through speakers or headphones
#### Simple analogy

**Audio processing is like:**

a sound editor that cleans, edits, and enhances everything your ears hear through a computer

___

### Types of audio processing

- **Analog Audio Processing** → works with real sound waves
- **Digital Audio Processing (DSP)** → works with digital signals
- **Real-time Processing** → happens instantly (calls, games)
- **Post-processing** → editing recorded audio
