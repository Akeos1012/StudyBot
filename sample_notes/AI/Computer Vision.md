**[[Computer Vision]]** – Computer vision refers to the **field of artificial intelligence that enables computers to interpret, analyze, and understand visual information from images and videos, similar to human vision**. It allows machines to extract meaningful data from visual inputs and make decisions based on what they “see.”

- Uses [[AI]] and [[Machine Learning]] to process visual data
- Works with images, videos, and real-time camera input
- Extracts features such as shapes, objects, and patterns
- Commonly uses [[Deep Learning]] models like neural networks
- Applied in facial recognition, object detection, and autonomous systems
- Relies on large-scale [[Data]] for training and accuracy improvement

Computer vision is a branch of artificial intelligence focused on enabling machines to understand and interpret visual information from the physical world.
