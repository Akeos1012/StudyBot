Mainframe - A **large, powerful [[Computer]] system designed to process massive amounts of data and support hundreds to thousands of users at the same time**, mainly used by governments, banks, and large corporations.

It is built for **high reliability, security, and continuous operation** (often running 24/7 without stopping).

- Processes large-scale transactions (banking, payments, airline bookings)
- Supports thousands of simultaneous users
- Runs critical business applications
- Ensures high security and data integrity
- Operates continuously with minimal downtime

#### Where it is used

- Banks and financial systems
- Government agencies
- Insurance companies
- Airlines and reservation systems
- Large enterprise data centers
#### Why it is important? 

- Handles mission-critical systems
- Processes millions of transactions per second
- Extremely reliable and fault-tolerant
- Supports legacy systems still used today
