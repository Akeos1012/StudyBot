**Arithmetic Operations** – These are <u>basic mathematical calculations</u> performed on numbers.

In computing and the CPU (specifically the **[[ALU]]**), arithmetic operations are used to process numerical data and produce results.

**Main arithmetic operations:**
- ➕ Addition
- ➖ Subtraction
- ✖️ Multiplication
- ➗ Division
#### Why it matters?

Every program that uses numbers, games, calculators, spreadsheets, [[AI]] models that relies on arithmetic operations happening billions of times inside the [[CPU]].
